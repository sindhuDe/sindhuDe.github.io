Global GDP Rankings Over Time

GDP growth trajectory for the top emitters in past years

Slide the bar below to see the progression




By moving the slider on the left, you can view the GDP growth rankings of the top 5 CO2-emitting G20 countries from 2019 over the past 10 years.


Studying these rankings reveals important insights into the economic paths of high-carbon-emitting nations.


By examining changes in GDP growth rankings, we can identify patterns and trends that provide deeper insights into the relationship between economic performance and carbon emissions worldwide.


China and India , as middle-income countries, show significant economic growth along with escalating CO2 emissions. With their ongoing industrialization and urbanization, it is essential to strike a balance between economic growth and environmental sustainability.



To thoroughly understand India and China, it's necessary to consider historical trends in population, employment rates, and inflation's impact on their economies. These factors are essential for grasping the complexities of the correlation between GDP growth and CO2 emissions.
